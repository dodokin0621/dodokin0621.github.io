            IN SOCIETY
I always focus on social issues and try to draw attention to these issues through my works. My works may not be solutions, but could be possibilities. My approach is giving my audience the opportunities to focus on these problems, letting them thinking through my speculation and working together to find a better way ingenious. I try to use my works to make people overcome their prejudices, unify and make them think about justice. My approach is giving my audience the opportunities to focus on these problems, letting them thinking through my speculation and working together to find a better way ingenious. It is not just a way to achieve a potential solution, but more about the conversations happened in between and collaboration process.

To attract my audience and let them participate in my works, I use satire as my design strategy. It is ironic which relieve some burden from the viewer let the viewer experience a dilemma: Is it serious or not? Real or not? Weird or not? Thus as a critical design, let my audience make up by their mind. I was influenced by lots of 90s comedy, which used a lot of satires. A skillful use of satire and irony can engage an audience in a more constructive way by appealing to the imagination as well as engaging the intellect.

Humor, as my other methodology could help to make an irony, it could use a format to stimulate audiences ideas and sensation, and it could be an excellent way to make the work contain double-take thinking. All of these are the further step for me to approach through my works. Especial most of my topics are serious issues connected to daily life; the humor be used here should express more to attack the issue rather than just make it enjoyable for the audience.

I am always inspired by unique user behaviors and way of narrative in post-digital age. Since I live in the post-digital age and want to use this special contemporary culture to communicate. I am first introduced to a concept that   irrelevant ingredients as an age of cultural, social and economic upheavals are driven to a large extent by computational digital technology.

Also, the digital in post-digital should not be understood in any technical-scientific or media theoretical sense, but rather in the way the term is broadly used in popular culture. What I am inspired by are this new culture and the ideologies behind it. For instance, most digital-generated contents based on Html and CSS language are more sincere and direct to an audience, since they could be directly used, copied, shared edited by users.

Nowadays, graphic design has more possibility,because of the internet work. I have interest on this kind of unreality. I find the connection and contrast between reality and impossibility, which in another way is the future world.  I speculate a future where, the more I will be driven by new strategy, the more I struggle for knowledge and for the recognition of the sacredness of nature.
